io.ox/mail/detail
The point for the detail view of a selected mail.
The baton is forwarded.
It is followed by points for each mail detail:

- io.ox/mail/detail/contact-picture - io.ox/mail/detail/receiveddate - io.ox/mail/detail/fromlist - io.ox/mail/detail/thread-position - io.ox/mail/detail/flag - io.ox/mail/detail/subject - io.ox/mail/detail/tocopy - io.ox/mail/detail/attachments - io.ox/mail/detail/inline-links - io.ox/mail/detail/phishing-warning - io.ox/mail/detail/externalresources-warning - io.ox/mail/detail/content
